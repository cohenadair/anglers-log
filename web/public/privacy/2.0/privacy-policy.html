Anglers' Log Privacy Policy

Introduction

Anglers' Log ("us", "we", or "our") operates anglerslog.ca, Anglers' Log for Android, and Anglers' Log for iOS (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of data we receive from people using the App ("User").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

This policy applies to App versions x.y.y, where "x" is any number greater than or equal to 2, and "y" is any number.

Information Gathered

The following section describes data gathered by the App, and may not apply to external libraries used within the App ("Third Parties"). Please review the "Third Parties" section of this document for Privacy Policies on individual Third Parties used in the App.

Fishing Data

All information ("Data") gathered by the App is stored locally on the device of the User. Data is neither sent to an online server, nor shared with a Third Party unless authorized by the User.

Location

A User's location is used for two reasons:

  1. To record and save new fishing locations, and
  2. Gather current weather and atmospheric information.
The App does not share a User's location with any Third Party; however, Third Parties used in the App may explicitly request a User's current location.

Storage Access

The App requires access to the User's device storage for two reasons:

  1. To save photos taken by the App, and
  2. To attach existing photos to Data.

Network & Internet

The App does not require a valid network or internet connection to function; however, the following features do require a valid network connection:

  • Automatic gathering of weather and atmospheric information.
  • Data restore and backup.
  • Data importing.
  • The loading and displaying of the world map.
In addition, certain Third Parties utilize the internet in order to improve User experience. See the "Third Parties" section of this policy for more information.

Third Parties

Firebase

Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics is used to send us detailed crash reports and anonymous App usage data. Please review the Firebase Privacy Policy here.

SendGrid

SendGrid allows the User to send us feedback emails from within the App. Please review the SendGrid Privacy Policy here.

RevenueCat

RevenueCat is a tool that allows us to easier manage App pro subscriptions. Please review the RevenueCat Privacy Policy here.

Google Drive

Google Drive integration within the App allows the User to backup their data to a private folder in their Google Drive account. This data is accessible only to the App and is not shared with any Third Party. The App uses the "drive.appdata" scope of the Google Drive API. Please review the App's OAuth Consent Screen, and Google Drive's Privacy Policy.
